Our Mission

Built for Nepal, Powered by Trust

We started with one idea: shopping in Nepal deserves to be simpler, faster, and more reliable. From the hills of Pokhara to the streets of Kathmandu, every order we fulfill is a promise kept.

We support local vendors, integrate homegrown payment gateways like eSewa and Khalti, and ensure your money stays in the Nepali ecosystem.

Our platform is built with transparency and quality in mind — no hidden fees, no counterfeit products, just genuine goods delivered to your doorstep.
